$s(t) = \int_0^t||r'(x)||dx$
$s(t) = \int_0^t\sqrt{(e^x + e^{-x})^2}dx$
$s(t) = \int_0^te^x + e^{-x}dx$
$s(t) = e^x - e^{-x}|^t_0$

$s(t) = e^t - e^{-t}$
